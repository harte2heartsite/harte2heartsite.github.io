About Brenda

Having worked as a speech coach for 20 years, I know what planning and stress can be involved in delivering a speech that will do you justice.    Indeed those who speak clearly will always have an advantage in life. 

With Harte2Heart all that anxiety experienced at weddings, business meetings,  interviews or indeed anywhere you  speak in public is minimised.   My courses are  successful.   You know well when you are handling the difficult task of public speaking to your satisfaction.    You make the decision to improve and let Harte2Heart do the rest!

I am based in Howth, Co. Dublin and  cover the Leinster area.
